# ❓ FAQ

### What is RDEV Hub?

A personal documentation hub showcasing my projects, tools, and Linux gaming experiments — all in one place, similar to a project wiki.

### What is the Binance AI Bot?

An experimental AI-powered trading bot for Binance Futures, written in Python. It's intended for learning and research purposes — **not financial advice**.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** Trading bots carry financial risk. Use at your own discretion and never trade with funds you can't afford to lose.

### What is the Linux Gaming Lab?

A separate site ([rdevlinux.netlify.app](https://rdevlinux.netlify.app/)) where I document game compatibility on Linux, Proton tweaks, and performance tuning notes.

### Can I contribute?

Yes! Check the [Developers](/developers) page for setup instructions and contribution guidelines.

### How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Open an issue on the relevant GitHub repository:

- [binance-ai-bot issues](https://github.com/rarl18/binance-ai-bot/issues)
- [RDEV hub issues](https://github.com/rarl18/RDEV/issues)
